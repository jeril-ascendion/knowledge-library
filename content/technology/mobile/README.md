## Overview

Mobile is the most exposed surface of any consumer or enterprise programme in 2026. The device is in the user's pocket, the store reviews are public, the regulatory regime is real, and the platform vendors set rules that shift each year. The Mobile Development hub captures seven disciplines that, taken together, define a production-grade mobile programme — platforms, architecture, quality, security, backend-data, delivery, enterprise — each as an independently consumable architecture reference with principles, technology trade-offs, implementation guidance, and named anti-patterns.

Use this as a body of knowledge during ADR discussions, as a checklist during architecture reviews, and as onboarding material for new mobile engineers and solutions architects. The flip-card index below summarises the key technologies, patterns, and quick stats per section; the section pages carry the substantive depth.
